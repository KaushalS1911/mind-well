Payment Terms

Mode of Payment

All payments must be made via online mode only. Accepted methods include UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and authorized payment gateways.

Advance Payment Requirement

  • Individual & Family Sessions: Full payment must be made in advance at the time of booking.

Individual & Family Counseling Sessions

Free Cancellation

Cancellations made at least 24 hours prior to the scheduled session are eligible for a full refund.

Rescheduling Policy

  • One-time rescheduling is allowed within 7 calendar days of the original session date.
  • Rescheduling must be requested at least 12 hours prior to the session.
  • After the first rescheduling, no refund will be provided for cancellations or missed sessions.

No-Show Policy

Failure to attend a session without prior notice will be considered a no-show. No refund or rescheduling will be permitted.

Capsule Courses

1. Individual Capsule Courses

Cancellations are eligible for a full refund if requested at least 7 days prior to the start of the first session.

No Refunds

Cancellations made within 7 days of the first session are non-refundable.

2. Group/Batch Capsule Courses

No Free Cancellation

Due to group scheduling and resource allocation, free cancellation is not permitted.

Partial Refund

A 50% refund is allowed if cancellation is requested at least 48 hours prior to the start of the first group session.

No Refunds Post Commencement

Once the group course has started, no refunds will be issued for any reason including withdrawal or absence.
